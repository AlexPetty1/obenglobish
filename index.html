Obenglobish Translator

Rules implemented:

1. Add ob in front of every vowel.
2. Add ob only in front of first of 2 double vowels.
3. Add ob in front of y if at end of a word.
4. Add ob in in front of y if between 2 consonants.
5. Dont add ob in front of e if at end of the word and the word has have another vowel.
